User Experience

In accordance with keeping our users safe online and in person. We have implemented certain rules that one must followed at all time. As was mentioned before, about valuing individuals, the reason for this criteria is for having the best user experience, while keeping you safe. When posting a thread, and then be awarded a certain number of points.By committing userself in a public post, you are leaving your intentions public. If for some reason you cannot make the commitment, then there will be a public offering of your points.

This has both good and bad consequences for users. The good is that you will be committed to keeping a safe and non-hostile envrionment. This has the consequences of taking out the processing fee and movie seating as well as the other users from your income. These consequences are only for those that cannot handle their differences through per-to-per messanging. Controlling of messaging is meant to staying committed to the website, other users, and yourself. A little on the messaging feature, in order to keep users safe, the member responding to a post of a potential movie date has control over the metting time, and setting. They are the only ones with access to this feature. After, if they enjoyed each others company, they can create a group of friends on the website to keep in contact.

Both users must register that they attended the event. After its over, both must click off the active feature on the mobile app that they have exited the event. Once clicked, they must not have further contact during that day in person. This only applies to the first couple of meetings. Users must engaged in at least 5 comitted meeting times and fill out their experiences each and every meeting engaement for this feature to be deactivated. If during that time this rule is violated in waheterv form, not including verifiable life or death situations, then their accounts will be deactivated and both banned from the site. This rule also applies to group meet ups.

The Enthusiast are those that have a little to no cime or theater experience, but don't feel quiet there yet for the other two categories.

The Fanatic, these are categorized as those that are into cult classics and know their way around a few kung fu movies.
Therefore, it's expected for both to know a little bit about the same types of movies so there won't any weird vibes when interacting if you choose to meet in person.

As with the Enthusiast, your messaging will be reduced depending on the committed events.

Unlimited Plan: (Critic) $8.99 per month

Last is the Critic. The critic category are those that are deeply involved in the film industry.
This is definied as registered to multiple sites, writing a bloq, following actors, or even things as going to conventions often.
So this subscription is pretty much for those that are are wanting to catch the next film sooner than later.

The major difference between this plan and the rest, is that you have unlimited messaging. However, if you cannot stay committed, you pay double the fee. So be wise.
